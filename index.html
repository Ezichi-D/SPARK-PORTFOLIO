About Me

Hi there — I’m Sparkle.

I’m a broadcaster and voice‑over artist who loves stories, conversations, and creating audio that connects with people.

I’ve worked in radio, television, and content production — from early training at TVC Communications to content writing at Max FM 102.3, and hosting and production roles at City FM 105.1 and Crowther 88.5FM. I’ve hosted shows, voiced promos, written scripts, and presented news — always aiming to communicate clearly and with heart.

I also run the “What’s Trending” podcast, where I explore pop culture, social topics, and stories that matter. Being a law graduate has also helped shape how I speak, think, and express ideas — with clarity and confidence.

This site is a snapshot of that journey so far — explore my work below!
